5. Plagiarism Policy

Zero Tolerance for Plagiarism

All content must be original or properly attributed. Plagiarism will result in immediate account suspension.

What Constitutes Plagiarism:

  • Copying text word-for-word without attribution
  • Rewriting someone else's work without credit
  • Using characters from other works without permission
  • Copying storylines from published works

Proper Attribution:

  • Use quotation marks for direct quotes
  • Cite author, title, and publication date
  • Acknowledge sources of inspiration
  • Mark public domain content clearly
